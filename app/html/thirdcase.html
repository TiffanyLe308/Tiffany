About Us

Bike Ltd started in 1982 with one goal: Create a better cycling experience for people around the world.
The founders of Bike were craftsmen and engineers. They understood their strengths. So to make cycling more enjoyable and accessible to people around the world, they did what they knew best.
They set out to improve the cycling experience by following three guiding principles: craftsmanship, innovation and inspiration.
Over the last three decades, Bike has grown well beyond its manufacturing roots to manufacture quality bicycles and gears.
